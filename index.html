FAQ

What is AroundMe?

AroundMe is an innovative proximity solution, we provide you with cloud-based services powered with physical devices hosted on your site.

How does it work?

We setup little Bluetooth-enabled devices on your site which you can control through your online account. You can instruct these smart little devices how to interact with your customers. Got a great new offer you wish more of your visitors knew about? not a problem! Login to your account & create a new campaign & assign it to one or more of your devices & let them do the rest for you! When customers walk by the device' zone, they get your offer in their notification bar, no custom or 3rd party mobile app needs to be preinstalled! and you can customize that notification with a logo & a title of your own. Do you collect feedback from your customers? then it's time to re-design that experience! create a survey-based campaign & let your customers submit their feedback through their mobiles phones. Slick, intuitive & your visitors will love it! AroundMe sends you the feedback via PDF reports & visual insights are available on your Dashboards.

Do I need to maintain the devices installed on my site?

No. AroundMe' team does a one-time setup installing & distributing these devices in your zone in locations of your preference. Once installed, you can do your campaigns online through the account provided to you, no further work is required on any device.

How do I pay for this service?

We provide a simple monthly pricing scheme where you pay per device. The price you pay is inclusive of all cloud-services you use, no extra or hidden fees will be charged.
